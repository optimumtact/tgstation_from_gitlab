plant graft
/obj/item/graft

A new subsystem for hydroponics, as a way to share specific traits into plants, as a way to phase out the DNA manipulator.

Vars

graft_appearanceDetermines the appearance of the graft. Rudimentary right now so it just picks randomly.
parent_seedSeed type that the graft was taken from, used for applying parent stats.
stored_traitThis stored the trait taken from the parent plant. Defaults to perenial growth.

Procs

get_graft_textAdds text to the plant analyzer which describes the graft's parent plant and any stored trait it has, if any.
