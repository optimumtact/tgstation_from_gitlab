19 May 2020

Garen7 updated:

  • Angelic juggernaut constructs can now use their punch spell on cultists.
  • Angelic juggernaut constructs' punch spell will now do the intended effect of 30 damage and a 5 second knockdown.
  • Added new effects for the angelic juggernaut construct's spells

Rodey125 updated:

  • gumballs and spider lollipops are now a subtype of chewables

  • The Clarke's weight has been reduced, causing it not to break floor tiles anymore.

  • You can now mine rock flora into volcanic ash to make glass.

bobbahbrown updated:

  • Jannies rejoice, the sanitation department has developed a new experimental broom module for our robotic cleaning friends. Ask your roboticist for a broom upgrade today!

  • cult leaders can no longer use final reckoning in narsie summon locations
